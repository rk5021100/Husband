📝 Game Rules

  • 🎯 Objective: Help all 3 couples (husbands and wives) cross the river safely to the right island.
  • 🚤 Boat: Can carry 1 or 2 characters at a time. Only characters on the same side as the boat can board or disembark.
  • 💔 Jealousy Rule: No wife can be left with another man unless her own husband is present. If this happens, the game ends in failure.
  • 👆 Tap: Tap characters to load/unload them from the boat.
  • 🏁 Win: Move all 6 characters to the right island without violating the jealousy rule.
